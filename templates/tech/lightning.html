With this in mind Lightning will enable an experience that's very much like a credit card except with lower transaction fees. Most credit card payments add a 1-3% fee, many times this is hidden in the price of items you buy. Saving costs is great but an even more important part of Lightning is that its design and development effort is going into making sure that when you buy something no one else is storing your entire shopping history. Today many companies collect our data and use it manipulate us into purchasing things we don't need and then sell our data to others who really shouldn't have it. Lightning will make it so that only the buyer and seller know each other and no other parties have access to the transaction. In that way Lightning will also work a lot like cash but on your phone. {% endblock vs_alts %}
