Bitcoin, not bitcoin) #} {% set sub_project="" %} {# set to False or "" if not using #} {% set simple_desc="More flexible and private Bitcoin smart contracts" %} {# Keep this short (~50-70 characters) #} {% set details=True %} {% set further_reading=True %} {% block at_a_glance %} {# This is the one required block #} Suppose you and 12 other business partners decide to collectively manage BTC for your startup. You all want an equal (or maybe unequal) say on what to spend your money on. You can store it all in one bank account and share a password, effectively trusting every person to not misbehave. Alternatively you can use a solution like Taproot to make sure you have equal (or unequal) say on when you can spend your BTC, effectively turning a business contract into an agreement enforced by the Bitcoin network. Taproot also improves privacy. Today if you and your business partners had such an agreement on the Bitcoin network and decided to spend your BTC on a new investment then the moment you did everyone on the Bitcoin network would know the wallets of every partner involved in the decision. This is bad if you don't want the world knowing the operations of your business. With Taproot (really Taproot + Schnorr + Taproot Scripts) you can make sure your transactions reveal much less or, in many cases, no information about your operations. {% endblock at_a_glance %} {% block status %} Taproot is currently a Bitcoin Improvement Proposal at draft status which means it may make its way into the bitcoind software. {% endblock status %} {% block future %} If Taproot is activated all sorts of new types of applications and money management will be available and enforceable by the Bitcoin network.
