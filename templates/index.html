Bitcoin has the hardest tech

Bitcoin is not flashy, nor hyped tech. Bitcoin is tech that gets serious work done well. Unfortunately this approach often leads to the erroneous and deceptive belief that Bitcoin is technologically archaic, or worse has no development.

As a former Bitcoin newbie myself I can tell you that I spent years viewing Bitcoin as old tech, rather than as tech focused on a singular and vital goal: a native internet currency, that may one day bring to money what the Internet brought to freedom of speech.
